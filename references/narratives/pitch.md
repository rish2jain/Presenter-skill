# Pitch Deck Narrative (12-slide arc)

Use with `**Purpose:** pitch` in outline front-matter.

## Golden thread

Each slide must follow logically from the previous ("therefore" test).

| # | Layout | Content |
|---|--------|---------|
| 1 | `title` | Company + one-line hook |
| 2 | `bullet-list` | Problem (the villain) — quantified pain |
| 3 | `bullet-list` | Solution (the hero) — what you do |
| 4 | `two-column-split` | Why now — market shift + chart |
| 5 | `stat-callout` | Traction — proof it works |
| 6 | `two-column-split` | Product — screenshot or demo |
| 7 | `stat-callout` | Business model / unit economics |
| 8 | `comparison` | Competition — why you win |
| 9 | `cards-3` | Team — founders + key hires |
| 10 | `timeline` | Roadmap — next 12–18 months |
| 11 | `table` or `stat-callout` | Financials / the ask |
| 12 | `closing` | Thank you + contact |

## Rules

- Max **15 slides** in main deck; detail goes in appendix (`gen_appendix.py`).
- Every stat needs `- Source:` or citation in `- Notes:`.
- Set `**Takeaway:**` in front-matter (one sentence investors should remember).
- VCs spend most time on **financials, team, traction** — make those slides dense with proof.
